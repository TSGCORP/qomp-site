§ About

Qomp is the software arm of TSG Executive Search & Compensation — an independent comp consulting firm based in Phoenix. Founded in 2021 and built on more than 30 years of executive search and compensation experience, TSG has spent the last few years helping mid-size employers run pay correctly. Qomp is what happens when decades of advisory expertise gets put into a dashboard.

§ 01 — The reason

Why we built Qomp — and why now.

With more than three decades in executive search and compensation consulting, we've watched mid-size employers struggle with the same problem for years: they need real compensation data and expert interpretation, but the industry that provides those things is built for the Fortune 500. Mercer engagements start at $30K. Radford requires survey participation and complex contracts. Pave is built for venture-backed tech.

The companies who needed comp clarity the most — healthcare, financial services, professional services in the 100–1,500 employee range — were the ones being ignored.

So they made do. They Googled salary ranges. They asked their network. They guessed. They lost good people to better offers they didn't know existed. They overpaid in some places and underpaid in others, with no way to tell which was which until it was too late.

When TSG was founded in 2021, the goal was to bring senior-level executive search and compensation expertise directly to mid-size employers — without the bureaucracy of the big firms. Then in 2024, Salary.com opened their CAMD dataset to partners. That changed everything. For the first time, the same data Fortune 500 comp teams use could power a product priced for the mid-market.

Qomp launches in 2026 because the conditions are finally right. The data is accessible. The HRIS APIs are mature enough. And the mid-size employers who've been ignored by the industry deserve the same intelligence everyone else has had for decades.

§ 02 — Principles

What we believe about compensation.

These aren't marketing values. They're operating commitments. Every product decision, pricing call, and advisory recommendation runs through these.

01
Data without interpretation is malpractice.
Comp software that hands you a dashboard and walks away has done half the job. The numbers don't tell you what to do. Every Qomp plan includes TSG advisors because reading a chart is easy; deciding what action to take is hard.
02
Mid-size companies aren't smaller enterprises.
They have different problems, different budgets, and different timelines than Fortune 500 companies. We refuse to build watered-down enterprise software. Qomp is designed from the ground up for HR teams of three to ten.
03
Crowdsourced data is for cocktail parties.
Self-reported salary data from anonymous internet users is fine for water-cooler curiosity. It's malpractice to make compensation decisions from it. Qomp uses HR-reported, validated data from 8,712 companies — and nothing else.
04
Transparency builds trust faster than mystery.
We publish our pricing. We disclose our data sources. We tell you what's included and what isn't. Pricing pages that say "contact us for a quote" are usually hiding something. Ours doesn't.

§ 04 — Founder
SR
Stuart Reeves
Founder & Principal
More than 30 years of experience in executive search, HR consulting, and compensation strategy. Founded TSG in 2021 to bring senior-level advisory expertise directly to mid-size employers. Based in Phoenix. Building Qomp because the mid-market deserves better.
For three decades I've watched HR leaders at real companies get treated like an afterthought by the comp industry. Qomp is my answer.

I started TSG in 2021 because I wanted to do executive search and compensation consulting the way I thought it should be done — directly, without the bureaucracy of the big firms, and priced so mid-size companies could actually afford it. By that point I'd already spent three decades in the field, and I knew exactly what was missing.

What I learned over those decades is that the work isn't the bottleneck. The data is the bottleneck. Without real benchmarks, comp advisors spend half their engagement just gathering market data — when they could be spending that time on actual interpretation and recommendations.

Qomp closes that gap. Live data flowing in automatically means our advisors can do the work they were trained to do — interpret, recommend, and guide decisions — instead of running surveys. That's why every Qomp plan includes TSG advisory time. The software handles the mechanics; the humans handle the judgment.
