Questions, answered.

The questions HR leaders ask before signing. Don't see yours? Just ask.

What if my company grows past my tier limit?
We move you to the next tier prorated to the day. No penalties, no awkward renegotiations. We'd rather you grow with us than feel boxed in.
How does the TSG advisory actually work?
Every quarter, your assigned TSG advisor reviews your dashboard and writes a short report — what's changed, where the risks are, what to do before your next merit cycle. Intelligence and Enterprise tiers also include direct email and Slack access for ad-hoc questions.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans cancel with 30 days notice. Annual plans don't auto-renew — we earn the renewal every twelve months. We don't believe in handcuffs.
What if my HRIS isn't on your integration list?
We can ingest a CSV export weekly while we build your specific integration — usually within 30 days. Tell us what you use and we'll prioritize it.
Is this just a Salary.com reseller?
No. We're a Salary.com partner with full CAMD data access — but the platform, the dashboard logic, the role matching engine, and the advisory layer are all built by us. Salary.com powers the data; Qomp is the product.
How is my employee data secured?
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're SOC 2 Type II in progress (audit completing Q3 2026). Your data is never sold, never shared with other clients, and never used to train external models.
