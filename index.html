§ 03 — How it works

Live in 48 hours.
Insightful forever.

Most comp tools take three months to onboard. Qomp connects to your HRIS through an API key, benchmarks every role against Salary.com, and delivers your first dashboard in two days. From there, TSG takes over.

Step 01
Connect your HRIS.
Generate an API key in BambooHR, Rippling, or ADP and paste it into Qomp. Your full roster syncs automatically.
Step 02
We benchmark every role.
Qomp matches your job titles to Salary.com's CAMD dataset using city-level geography and industry cuts.
Step 03
Your dashboard stays current.
Market data refreshes monthly. New hires sync nightly. You always see today's picture — never last year's.
Step 04
TSG interprets it for you.
Quarterly written insight reports from TSG's advisors. What the data means. What to do next.
